Simple Audio Importer — Mod Builder

Builds a ready-to-install Factorio mod from your audio files. No coding required. Everything runs locally in your browser.

Supported formats: .wav and .ogg only. Factorio does not support .mp3. Convert first if needed: CloudConvert.

Where to place the .zip file:
Windows %APPDATA%\Factorio\mods
Linux ~/.factorio/mods
macOS ~/Library/Application Support/factorio/mods
Also make sure Simple Audio Importer is installed in that same folder.
Your sounds will appear in the Programmable Speaker under the instrument Simple Audio Importer.
