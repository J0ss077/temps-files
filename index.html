Simple Audio Importer — Mod Builder

This tool builds a ready-to-use Factorio mod .zip from your audio files.
No coding required. Everything runs in your browser — no files are uploaded anywhere.

⚠️ Supported formats: .wav and .ogg only. Factorio does not support .mp3. Convert your files first if needed (CloudConvert).

Step 3 — Generate and install

Where to put the .zip:
C:\Users\YourUsername\AppData\Roaming\Factorio\mods
Tip (Windows): open File Explorer, click the address bar, type %APPDATA%\Factorio\mods and press Enter.

Make sure Simple Audio Importer is also installed in that same mods folder.
Your sounds will appear inside the Programmable Speaker under the instrument "Simple Audio Importer".
